Local Food Discovery Guide

Website Project Site Plan

Project Overview

The Local Food Discovery Guide is a web application designed to help users find local food options in their area. The site will feature a user-friendly interface that allows users to search for local restaurants, farmers' markets, and food events. The goal is to promote local food culture and support small businesses.

This site plan outlines the structure and functionality of the Local Food Discovery Guide.

Optional Domain: localfoodguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Local Food discovery website is to help users explore local foods and restaurants easily. The site will provide information about meals, recommended places to eat, and tips for discovering new foods. It will also include dynamic features such as location-based suggestions and weather-based food recommendations.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website includes people who enjoy exploring new foods, tourists looking for local dining options, and residents who want to discover restaurants in their area.

Scenarios

  • What restaurants near me serve popular local meals?
  • What food would be good to eat based on today's weather?
  • Where can I discover new meals I haven't tried before?

APIs and Dynamic Features

Geolocation API:

Used to detect the user's location and provide location-based suggestions.

Weather API:

Used to fetch current weather information and provide weather-based food recommendations.

Local JSON File

Restaurant information such as images, names, and descriptions will be stored in a JSON file and loaded dynamically on the restaurants page.
